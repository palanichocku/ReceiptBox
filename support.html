Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a receipt? ‚ñº
Simply tap the camera icon on the main screen, point your camera at the receipt, and tap the capture button. ReceiptBox will automatically detect the receipt edges and extract key information like date, merchant, and amount.
Is my data backed up? ‚ñº
Yes! ReceiptBox automatically syncs your receipts to iCloud, so they're backed up and accessible across all your Apple devices. You can disable this in Settings if you prefer local-only storage.
Can I export my receipts for tax purposes? ‚ñº
Absolutely! You can export receipts as PDF or CSV files. Go to Reports, select your date range and categories, then tap the Export button. This makes it easy to share with your accountant or import into tax software.
How accurate is the OCR text recognition? ‚ñº
Our OCR technology is highly accurate for most printed receipts. However, you can always manually edit any extracted information. Make sure to capture receipts in good lighting and hold your phone steady for best results.
Can I create custom expense categories? ‚ñº
Yes! In addition to default categories like Groceries, Transportation, and Entertainment, you can create unlimited custom categories in Settings > Categories. This helps you organize receipts according to your specific needs.
Does ReceiptBox work offline? ‚ñº
Yes! You can scan and view receipts offline. The app will sync your data with iCloud when you're back online. OCR processing requires an internet connection for the best accuracy.
Is there a limit to how many receipts I can store? ‚ñº
The free version allows up to 50 receipts per month. ReceiptBox Pro offers unlimited receipt storage, advanced search features, and priority support. Check the app for current pricing.
How do I delete a receipt? ‚ñº
Swipe left on any receipt in your list and tap the delete icon. You can also open a receipt and tap the trash icon in the top right corner. Deleted receipts are moved to the trash and permanently deleted after 30 days.
Can I share receipts with others? ‚ñº
Yes! Open any receipt and tap the share button to send it via email, message, or save to Files. You can share individual receipts or entire expense reports.
What iOS version do I need? ‚ñº
ReceiptBox requires iOS 15.0 or later. The app is optimized for all iPhone models from iPhone 8 and newer, and also works great on iPad.
