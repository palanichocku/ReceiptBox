Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: November 25, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to ReceiptBox. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

By using ReceiptBox, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Receipt Data: Images of receipts, extracted text including merchant names, dates, amounts, and categories
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email address and username
  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure within the app

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and crash reports
  • Location Data: We do not collect location data unless you explicitly grant permission for location-based features

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the ReceiptBox service
  • To process and store your receipt data
  • To perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition) on receipt images
  • To sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • To improve and optimize app performance
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or fraud
  • To send important updates about the app (if you opt-in)

Data Storage and Security

iCloud Storage

ReceiptBox uses Apple's iCloud service to sync your receipt data across your devices. When you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your receipt data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

Local Storage

Receipt data is stored locally on your device in a secure, encrypted format. This data remains on your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted local storage on your device
  • Secure authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app:

  • OCR Services: Receipt images may be processed by third-party OCR services to extract text
  • Analytics: We use analytics services to understand app usage and improve performance
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For users who opt-in to cloud features beyond iCloud

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from authorities.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your stored receipt data within the app
  • Correction: Edit or correct any receipt information
  • Deletion: Delete individual receipts or all your data at any time
  • Export: Export your receipt data in PDF or CSV format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics, iCloud sync, or other optional features

To exercise these rights, you can use the built-in features in the app or contact us at privacy@receiptbox.app

Children's Privacy

ReceiptBox is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using ReceiptBox, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@receiptbox.app
  • Support: support@receiptbox.app
  • Website: www.receiptbox.app

Consent

By using ReceiptBox, you consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.
